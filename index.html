About Me

I’m Naveen Roy, a dedicated web designer and developer with over 2 years of experience in creating stunning, user-friendly websites. Holding a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, I am driven by a passion for combining creativity and functionality in each project.

My expertise includes front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, along with a strong foundation in UI/UX design principles. I strive to build websites that not only look beautiful but are also optimized for performance, accessibility, and SEO.

My approach to each project is detail-oriented and customer-focused, ensuring that every website reflects my client’s vision while delivering a seamless user experience.

I believe in continuous learning and staying updated with the latest design trends, aiming to bring innovation and excellence to every project.
